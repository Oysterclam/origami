I really like this kind of structure. Motif? The curved-ness coming out of straight lines. It reminds me of calculus class with that idea.

Kind of hinting at curves while still maintaining the traditional straight "origami-ness" of folds. like approximating things. stating you could go on to infinity if you really wanted, but you are confident enough with this part of the way there.I like this kind of stuff.

The premise behind these is repeatedly crimp folding. Usually people use the crimps to make bends in appendages like legs or necks, but I think they look very pleasing when extended wider over an open surface of paper
